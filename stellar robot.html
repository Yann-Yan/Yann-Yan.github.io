Technology Strength

Proficient In Arduino Software Programming

We use Arduino as the main control board of the product, which integrates infrared tracking sensor, color sensor, ultrasonic ranging module, L298P motor driver chip, multiple steering gear and Bluetooth communication module. We connect all the modules together through programming, and also design highly accurate infrared tracking algorithm, ultrasonic ranging algorithm and color recognition algorithm to achieve high performance, high efficiency and high accuracy of the product.
